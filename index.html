Key features

MinuteGlass keeps the main experience focused on four controls: the dial, colors, appearance, and duration presets.

01

Touch-operated dial timer

Drag the large analog-style dial with your finger to set the timer visually, then start or pause from the same focused screen.

02

Theme colors

Choose from soft color themes including Blue, Mint, Lavender, Pink, Peach, and Gray.

03

Light and dark mode

Follow the system appearance or choose Light or Dark mode directly from the app settings.

04

Duration presets

Start quickly from 5, 10, 15, 20, 25, 30, 45, or 60 minutes.

Feature walkthrough

These screenshots come directly from the iOS Simulator and show the timer itself changing across themes and appearance modes.

Theme colors

The color setting changes the whole timer atmosphere, not just a small accent. Blue, Mint, Lavender, Pink, Peach, and Gray each keep the dial readable while changing the mood of the screen.

Light and dark mode

Appearance mode changes the same timer surface for bright or low-light use. MinuteGlass can follow the system setting or stay fixed in Light or Dark.
